# AI Lead Qualification Agent

## Overview

An AI-powered lead qualification system built using n8n, OpenAI GPT-4o Mini, Google Sheets, and Gmail.

The workflow automatically analyzes incoming leads, determines their purchase intent, and classifies them as High, Medium, or Low priority. Qualified leads trigger automated notifications, allowing businesses to focus on opportunities that are most likely to convert.

---

## Problem

Businesses often receive large numbers of inquiries, but not every lead has the same value. Sales teams spend significant time manually reviewing submissions to determine which prospects deserve immediate attention.

This process is slow, inconsistent, and difficult to scale.

---

## Solution

This workflow uses AI to automatically evaluate lead intent and prioritize incoming opportunities.

The system:

* Captures new lead submissions
* Extracts lead information
* Uses GPT-4o Mini to classify intent
* Assigns High, Medium, or Low priority
* Sends notifications only for qualified leads
* Filters out low-quality or irrelevant inquiries
